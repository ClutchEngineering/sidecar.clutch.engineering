# About the Honda Civic

The Honda Civic is one of the most iconic and enduring compact cars in automotive history. First introduced in 1972, the Civic has evolved through eleven generations to become synonymous with reliability, fuel efficiency, and practicality.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Honda Civic support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year:

**Wired Support (2016-2021):**
- Apple CarPlay and Android Auto were introduced in 2016
- Required USB cable connection
- Standard on most configurations by 2019

**Wireless Support (2022+):**
- **Higher configurations**: Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- **Base and mid-level configurations**: Wired Apple CarPlay and Android Auto only

All 2022 and newer Civic models feature a 7-inch or 9-inch touchscreen (depending on configuration) with the latest Honda infotainment system that supports smartphone integration.
