# About the Kia K5

The Kia K5 is a midsize sedan that replaced the Kia Optima in 2021, offering a bold design, modern technology features, and strong value. Known for its sporty styling and well-equipped interior, the K5 competes in the heart of the midsize sedan segment with an emphasis on connectivity and driver engagement.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Kia K5 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Kia K5 supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with wireless connectivity available on most configurations, though screen size determines the connection type on certain model years.

**2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 12.3-inch touchscreen display standard across all configurations
- Represents a significant upgrade with universal wireless connectivity

**2024 Models:**
- **8-inch touchscreen**: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (standard on base configurations)
- **10.25-inch touchscreen**: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (available on higher configurations)
- Note: The larger screen paradoxically requires a wired connection

**2023 Models:**
- **8-inch touchscreen**: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- **10.25-inch touchscreen**: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Screen size determines wireless vs wired connectivity

**2022 Models:**
- **8-inch touchscreen**: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- **10.25-inch touchscreen**: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (available on GT-Line, EX, and GT configurations)
- Some units received software updates enabling wireless functionality

**2021 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available on most configurations with 8-inch touchscreen
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto on configurations with available 10.25-inch touchscreen
- First model year of the K5 nameplate, replacing the Optima

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
