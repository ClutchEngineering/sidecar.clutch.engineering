# About the Mazda 3

The Mazda 3 is a compact sedan and hatchback known for its sporty handling, refined design, and driver-focused features. First introduced in 2003, the Mazda 3 has become one of the brand's most popular models worldwide.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Mazda 3 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Mazda 3 supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with factory support starting in 2019. Wireless connectivity became available starting with the 2024 model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available on configurations with 10.25-inch touchscreen
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto on configurations with 8.8-inch touchscreen
- Wireless charging pad available on configurations with the larger screen
- USB Type-C charging ports

**2019-2023 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all configurations
- 8.8-inch infotainment display
- USB connection required for smartphone integration
- First model year with factory Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support

**2014-2018 Models:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Retrofit available through Mazda dealerships for models equipped with the MZD Connect infotainment system
- Aftermarket solutions available

**2013 and Earlier:**
- No Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket head unit replacement required for smartphone integration

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
