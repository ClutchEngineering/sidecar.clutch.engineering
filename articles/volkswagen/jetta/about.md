# About the Volkswagen Jetta

The Volkswagen Jetta is a compact sedan that has been a staple of the automotive market since 1979. Known for its blend of European handling, practicality, and value, the Jetta remains one of Volkswagen's best-selling models in North America.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Volkswagen Jetta support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Volkswagen Jetta supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with connectivity options varying by model year:

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available on most configurations
- 6.5-inch touchscreen on base models
- 8-inch or 8.5-inch touchscreen on higher configurations
- The 2025 SE and SEL trims feature wireless connectivity as standard equipment

**2022-2023 Models:**
- **Mixed availability**: Lower trims (S, Sport, SE) feature wired-only connectivity with the MIB2 infotainment system and 6.5-inch displays
- **Higher trims** (SEL, GLI): Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto with the MIB3 infotainment system and 8-inch displays
- Wireless phone charging available on models equipped with wireless connectivity

**2021 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto introduced on higher trims with the MIB3 infotainment system
- Wired connectivity available on lower trims with MIB2 system

**2018-2020 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Available on most configurations through the MIB2 infotainment system
- Standard or available depending on trim level

**2016-2017 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Initially limited to higher trims through the MIB II infotainment system
- 2016 marked the introduction of smartphone integration to the Jetta lineup

**2015 and Earlier:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions may be available for adding smartphone integration

For models with wired-only connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
