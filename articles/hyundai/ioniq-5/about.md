# About the Hyundai IONIQ 5

The Hyundai IONIQ 5 is an all-electric SUV that showcases Hyundai's innovative approach to electric vehicle design and technology. Launched in 2021, the IONIQ 5 is built on Hyundai's dedicated Electric-Global Modular Platform (E-GMP) and features distinctive retro-futuristic styling inspired by the classic Hyundai Pony.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the IONIQ 5 support wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Support for wireless smartphone integration varies by model year:

**2025+ Model Year:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay
- **Wireless** Android Auto
- Standard across all configurations
- Dual 12.3-inch displays (digital instrument cluster and touchscreen)
- Wireless smartphone charging pad (standard on higher configurations)

**2022-2024 Model Years:**
- **Wired-only** Apple CarPlay (USB-A connection required)
- **Wired-only** Android Auto (USB-A connection required)
- Aftermarket wireless adapters can be purchased to add wireless functionality

If wireless connectivity is important to you, the 2025 model year represents a significant upgrade with native wireless support for both platforms.
