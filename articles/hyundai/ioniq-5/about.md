# About the Hyundai IONIQ 5

The Hyundai IONIQ 5 is an all-electric SUV built on Hyundai's Electric-Global Modular Platform (E-GMP). Launched as a 2022 model, the IONIQ 5 features distinctive retro-futuristic styling inspired by the classic Hyundai Pony.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Hyundai IONIQ 5 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, all IONIQ 5 model years support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with wireless connectivity introduced starting in 2025:

**2025-2026 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Standard across all configurations
- Dual 12.3-inch displays (digital instrument cluster and touchscreen infotainment system)
- Next-generation infotainment system

**2022-2024 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB-A connection required)
- Dual 12.3-inch displays (digital instrument cluster and touchscreen infotainment system)
- Aftermarket wireless adapters available to add wireless functionality

The 2025 model year introduced native wireless support for both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto after Hyundai resolved earlier technical limitations. For 2022-2024 models with wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.
