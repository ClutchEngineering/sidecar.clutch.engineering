# About the Hyundai Sonata

The Hyundai Sonata is a midsize sedan known for its value, comfort, and modern technology features. First introduced in 1985, the Sonata has evolved into one of Hyundai's flagship models, offering a blend of style, efficiency, and advanced connectivity features.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Hyundai Sonata support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Hyundai Sonata supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability varying by model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 12.3-inch touchscreen display
- The 2024 model year marked a significant update with the introduction of standard wireless connectivity across all configurations

**2020-2023 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- 8-inch touchscreen on base SE configurations
- 10.25-inch touchscreen available on higher configurations
- Select 2020 models with 8-inch screens received a software update enabling wireless connectivity, though most models in this range remain wired-only

**2017-2019 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- USB cable connection required
- Standard on most configurations

**2015-2016 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- First introduced on 2015 models via software update for vehicles equipped with 8-inch navigation screen
- Became factory-standard on 2016 models built after January 15, 2016
- Earlier 2016 models required software update

**2014 and Earlier:**
- No Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions may be available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
