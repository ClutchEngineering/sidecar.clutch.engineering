# About the Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 is a compact SUV that has been a pioneer in the crossover segment since its introduction in 1994. Known for its versatility, reliability, and capability, the RAV4 remains one of the best-selling vehicles in North America.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Toyota RAV4 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Toyota RAV4 supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with wireless connectivity becoming standard starting with the 2023 model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all models
- 8-inch touchscreen on base models
- 10.5-inch touchscreen available on higher configurations
- Toyota Audio Multimedia system with enhanced connectivity features

**2023 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all models
- 8-inch or 10.5-inch touchscreen depending on configuration
- Major technology update introduced this model year

**2020-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Available through Entune 3.0 system
- 7-inch, 8-inch, or 9-inch touchscreen depending on model year and configuration
- Standard across all models

**2019 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay only via Entune 3.0 system
- Android Auto was not available from the factory on 2019 models
- 7-inch or 8-inch touchscreen depending on configuration

**2018 and Earlier:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- 2014-2018 models with Entune 2.0 or Entune 2.5 systems do not include smartphone integration
- Aftermarket solutions available for older models

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.

For the latest specifications and features on current RAV4 models, visit the [official Toyota RAV4 page](https://www.toyota.com/rav4/).
