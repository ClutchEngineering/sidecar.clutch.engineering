# About the Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 is a compact SUV that has been a pioneer in the crossover segment since its introduction in 1994. As one of the best-selling SUVs in North America, the RAV4 has built a reputation for exceptional reliability, practicality, and fuel efficiency. Starting with the 2026 model year, Toyota is making the RAV4 exclusively hybrid-powered across all trim levels, marking a significant milestone in the model's evolution and reinforcing Toyota's commitment to electrification while maintaining the versatility and capability that have made the RAV4 a perennial favorite.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What trim levels are available for the Toyota RAV4?

The Toyota RAV4 is offered in multiple trim levels to suit different needs and preferences:

**2025 Gasoline Models:**

- **LE**: The base trim offering excellent value with essential features, including an 8-inch touchscreen, Toyota Safety Sense 2.5, and a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing 203 horsepower. Starting MSRP is $29,800.

- **XLE**: Adds comfort and convenience features including upgraded upholstery, power driver's seat, and enhanced interior materials.

- **XLE Premium**: Builds on the XLE with a power moonroof, 19-inch alloy wheels, and heated front seats for added comfort.

- **Limited**: The top gasoline trim featuring a 10.5-inch Toyota Audio Multimedia touchscreen, JBL 11-speaker premium audio system, panoramic roof, digital rearview mirror, and heated steering wheel. Starting MSRP is $39,505.

**2025 Hybrid Models:**

Hybrid versions are available in multiple configurations including LE, XLE, XLE Premium, Limited, SE, and XSE trims. The Woodland Edition offers enhanced off-road styling and capability.

**2026 Models (Hybrid-Only):**

For 2026, all RAV4 models come exclusively with hybrid powertrains, available with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive depending on the trim level. The plug-in hybrid variant continues to offer even greater efficiency and performance.

[View current RAV4 models on Toyota.com](https://www.toyota.com/rav4/)

### Is the Toyota RAV4 hybrid? Does it come with all-wheel drive?

**The 2026 and newer Toyota RAV4 models are exclusively hybrid.** Following the success of hybrid variants representing over half of RAV4 sales in 2024, Toyota discontinued traditional gasoline-only engine options for the 2026 model year, making the entire RAV4 lineup electrified.

**Powertrain Options:**

**Standard Hybrid (2025 Models):**
- 2.5-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder engine paired with electric motors
- Front motor: 179 hp, 199 lb-ft torque
- Rear motor (AWD): 53 hp, 89 lb-ft torque
- Combined system output: 219 horsepower
- Standard all-wheel drive on most hybrid trims
- Fuel economy: Up to 41 city / 38 highway / 39 combined MPG

**2026 Hybrid Models:**
- Front-Wheel Drive: 226 horsepower
- All-Wheel Drive: 236 horsepower
- Fuel economy: Up to 44 MPG combined (FWD models)

**All-Wheel Drive Availability:**

The RAV4 offers Electronic On-Demand AWD, which uses an independent rear electric motor to provide power to the rear wheels when needed. This system enhances traction in various conditions while maintaining efficiency. AWD is standard on most hybrid trims and available as an option on others.

**Gasoline Models (2025 and earlier):**
- 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine
- Output: 203 horsepower, 184 lb-ft torque
- Eight-speed automatic transmission
- Available with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive

[Learn more about RAV4 hybrid technology](https://www.toyota.com/rav4/)

### What is the RAV4 Prime and how does it differ from the regular hybrid?

The **Toyota RAV4 Prime** (marketed as RAV4 Plug-In Hybrid starting in 2025) is a plug-in hybrid variant that combines electric-only driving capability with the flexibility of a gasoline engine for longer trips.

**Key Specifications:**

**Electric Range:**
- EPA-estimated 42 miles of all-electric driving range
- Total driving range: Approximately 600 miles combining electric and gasoline

**Power and Performance:**
- Combined output: 302 horsepower (2025), 320 horsepower (2026)
- 0-60 mph: 5.9 seconds
- Standard all-wheel drive
- One of the quickest vehicles in the compact SUV segment

**Fuel Efficiency:**
- 94 MPGe combined in EV-hybrid mode
- 38 MPG combined in gas-hybrid mode

**Charging:**
- Battery: 18.1 kWh
- Level 2 charging (240V): 2.5-4.5 hours for full charge
- Level 1 charging (120V): Approximately 12 hours

**Pricing:**
- 2025 SE trim: Starting at $45,615
- 2025 XSE trim: Starting at $49,485

The RAV4 Prime offers more power and the ability to drive on electricity alone for daily commutes, while the standard RAV4 Hybrid provides excellent efficiency without the need for plug-in charging.

### How reliable is the Toyota RAV4?

The Toyota RAV4 is exceptionally reliable, consistently ranking among the most dependable compact SUVs. The RAV4 has earned a **4.0 out of 5.0 reliability rating from RepairPal**, ranking **3rd out of 26** in the compact SUV category.

**Key Reliability Metrics:**
- Average annual repair cost: $429 (well below the compact SUV average)
- Unscheduled repair frequency: 0.3 times per year (equal to segment average, better than overall vehicle average of 0.4)
- Probability of severe issue: 10% (better than 11% for compact SUVs and 12% for all vehicles)
- Strong owner satisfaction ratings across multiple model years

**Consumer Reports** gives the 2025 RAV4 above-average predicted reliability, with an expected reliability much better than the average new car. This prediction is based on data from 2022, 2023, and 2024 models. The 2026 RAV4 receives a "New Models Predicted Reliability" rating of 86 out of 100.

Toyota as a brand ranks 3rd out of 22 manufacturers for new-car reliability according to Consumer Reports, reinforcing the RAV4's dependable pedigree.

The RAV4's reputation for longevity is well-earned, with many owners reporting trouble-free ownership well beyond 200,000 miles with proper maintenance. This exceptional durability makes the RAV4 an excellent choice for buyers prioritizing long-term reliability and low ownership costs.

[Read RepairPal's RAV4 reliability ratings](https://repairpal.com/reliability/toyota/rav4)

### Does the Toyota RAV4 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year and trim level:

**2025 Models:**
- Base trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 8-inch touchscreen
- Premium trims: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available as upgrade
- Higher trim levels feature a 10.5-inch touchscreen with enhanced connectivity

**2022-2024 Models:**
- Most trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Some upper trims: **Wireless** connectivity available
- 8-inch or 9-inch touchscreen depending on trim

**2019-2021 Models:**
- All trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Available through Entune 3.0 system
- Standard across most trim levels

**2018 and Earlier:**
- Limited or no smartphone integration support
- 2014-2018 models with Entune 2.0 system do not include factory CarPlay or Android Auto
- Aftermarket solutions available for older models

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto became standard on most RAV4 trims starting with the 2019 model year. For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without vehicle modification.

### What safety features come standard on the Toyota RAV4?

All 2025 Toyota RAV4 models come standard with **Toyota Safety Sense 2.5 (TSS 2.5)**, Toyota's comprehensive suite of advanced driver assistance systems. The RAV4 has earned strong safety ratings, including a **5-Star Overall Safety Rating from NHTSA** and top scores from the IIHS.

**Toyota Safety Sense 2.5 includes:**

- **Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian and Bicycle Detection**: Detects vehicles, pedestrians, and cyclists in your path, providing visual and audio alerts and automatic emergency braking if a collision is imminent.

- **Full-Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control**: Maintains a set speed and following distance from the vehicle ahead, working in stop-and-go traffic. Includes four adjustable following distance settings.

- **Lane Tracing Assist**: Helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane with active steering assistance when Dynamic Radar Cruise Control is active.

- **Road Sign Assist**: Recognizes and displays important road signs including speed limits, stop signs, and yield signs on the instrument cluster.

- **Automatic High Beams**: Automatically toggles between high and low beams based on surrounding traffic to maximize visibility without blinding other drivers.

- **Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist**: Warns the driver when unintentionally drifting from the lane and can provide gentle steering corrections.

**Additional Standard Safety Features:**
- Vehicle Stability Control and Traction Control
- Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brake-force Distribution (EBD)
- Brake Assist
- Smart Stop Technology
- 8 airbags (including driver and front passenger knee airbags)

**NHTSA Safety Ratings (2025):**
- Overall: 5 stars
- Frontal Crash: 4 stars
- Side Crash: 5 stars
- Rollover: 4 stars

**IIHS Ratings:**
- Crashworthiness: "Good" (highest rating) for front and side crashes
- Ratings apply to 2019-2025 models (current generation)

The RAV4's comprehensive safety suite comes standard across all trim levels, ensuring every model offers the same high level of protection regardless of price point.
