# About the Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 is a compact SUV that has been a pioneer in the crossover segment since 1994. Starting with the 2026 model year, Toyota is making the RAV4 exclusively hybrid-powered across all trim levels, reinforcing Toyota's commitment to electrification.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Toyota RAV4 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year and configuration:

**2025 Models:**
- Base configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 8-inch touchscreen
- Higher configurations: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available as upgrade
- Some configurations feature a 10.5-inch touchscreen with enhanced connectivity

**2022-2024 Models:**
- Most configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Some higher configurations: **Wireless** connectivity available
- 8-inch or 9-inch touchscreen depending on configuration

**2019-2021 Models:**
- All configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Available through Entune 3.0 system
- Standard across most configurations

**2018 and Earlier:**
- Limited or no smartphone integration support
- 2014-2018 models with Entune 2.0 system do not include factory CarPlay or Android Auto
- Aftermarket solutions available for older models

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto became standard on most RAV4 models starting with the 2019 model year. For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without vehicle modification.
