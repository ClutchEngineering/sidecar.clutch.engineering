# About the Toyota Tundra

The Toyota Tundra is a full-size pickup truck built in San Antonio, Texas, representing Toyota's commitment to the full-size truck segment with legendary reliability. For the 2022 model year, Toyota introduced an all-new third-generation Tundra featuring twin-turbocharged V6 engines, including Toyota's first hybrid powertrain in a Tundra, modern technology, and significantly improved towing capacity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Toyota Tundra support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support on the Toyota Tundra varies by model year:

**2025 Tundra:**
- **All configurations**: Standard Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- **Base configurations**: 8-inch touchscreen with **wired** connection (USB required)
- **Higher configurations**: 14-inch touchscreen with **wired** connection standard, **wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available on select configurations

**2022-2024 Tundra:**
- All configurations include Apple CarPlay and Android Auto as standard features
- Most configurations require a **wired** USB connection
- 8-inch touchscreen on base configurations, 14-inch touchscreen available on higher configurations

**2020-2021 Tundra (Second Generation):**
- Apple CarPlay and Android Auto added as standard features starting with the 2020 model year
- **Wired** connection required (USB cable)
- Available across all configurations with compatible infotainment system

**2014-2019 Tundra:**
- Factory Apple CarPlay and Android Auto **not available**
- Vehicles equipped with Entune 2.0 system do not support smartphone integration
- Aftermarket wireless CarPlay/Android Auto upgrade modules are available that integrate with the factory touchscreen without requiring screen replacement

**Pre-2014 Tundra:**
- No factory smartphone integration support
- Aftermarket head unit replacement required for CarPlay/Android Auto functionality

For Tundra models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto functionality without vehicle modification.
