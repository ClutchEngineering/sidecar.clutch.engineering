# About the Toyota Tundra

The Toyota Tundra is a full-size pickup truck that has been a cornerstone of Toyota's truck lineup since 1999, known for its durability and reliability.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Toyota Tundra support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support on the Toyota Tundra varies by model year:

**2022-2025 Models (Third Generation):**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all models
- 8-inch touchscreen standard, 14-inch touchscreen available
- USB-C ports for charging

**2020-2021 Models (Second Generation):**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard (USB connection required)
- 8-inch touchscreen with Toyota Audio Multimedia system
- Standard across all models

**2014-2019 Models:**
- Factory Apple CarPlay and Android Auto **not available**
- Equipped with Entune 2.0 system without smartphone integration
- Aftermarket upgrade modules available that integrate with the factory touchscreen to add wireless or wired CarPlay/Android Auto

**Pre-2014 Models:**
- No factory smartphone integration support
- Aftermarket head unit replacement required for CarPlay/Android Auto functionality

For 2020-2021 models with wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters can be added by plugging into the existing USB port, converting the system to wireless operation without vehicle modification.
