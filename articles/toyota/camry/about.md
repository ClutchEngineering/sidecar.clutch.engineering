# About the Toyota Camry

The Toyota Camry is a midsize sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability, comfortable ride, and practicality. Starting with the 2025 model year, Toyota made the Camry exclusively hybrid, marking a significant shift in the model's powertrain strategy.

## ECU map

- `700`: Engine control module (ECM)
- `701`: Transmission control module (TCM)

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Toyota Camry support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year and configuration:

**2025+ Models:**
- Base configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 8-inch touchscreen
- Higher configurations: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 12.3-inch touchscreen

**2021-2024 Models:**
- All configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- 8-inch or 9-inch touchscreen depending on configuration

**2018-2020 Models:**
- All configurations: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Available through Entune 3.0 system

**2015-2017 Models:**
- Limited or no smartphone integration support
- Aftermarket solutions available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.
