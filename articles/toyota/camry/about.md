# About the Toyota Camry

The Toyota Camry is a midsize sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability, comfortable ride, and practicality. As one of the best-selling vehicles in North America for decades, the Camry has built a reputation as a dependable family sedan that balances efficiency, comfort, and value. Starting with the 2025 model year, Toyota made the Camry exclusively hybrid, marking a significant shift in the model's powertrain strategy while maintaining the qualities that have made it a perennial favorite.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What trim levels are available for the Toyota Camry?

The Toyota Camry is offered in four main trim levels, each catering to different preferences:

- **LE**: The base trim offering excellent value with all essential features, including an 8-inch touchscreen, Toyota Safety Sense 3.0, and the hybrid powertrain. Starting MSRP is $28,400.

- **SE**: Adds sportier styling with 18-inch black alloy wheels, sport-tuned suspension, and more aggressive exterior design elements. This trim focuses on a more engaging driving experience while maintaining efficiency. Starting MSRP is $30,700.

- **XLE**: The comfort-oriented premium trim featuring heated front seats, a leather and microfiber interior, 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, and 12.3-inch touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. This trim emphasizes luxury and convenience features.

- **XSE**: The top-tier trim combining luxury and sport, featuring 19-inch alloy wheels, sport-tuned suspension, dual chrome exhaust tips, and all the premium features from the XLE. Starting MSRP is $35,695.

All-wheel drive is available as a $1,525 option on every trim level.

[View current Camry models on Toyota.com](https://www.toyota.com/camry/)

### Is the Toyota Camry hybrid? Does it come with all-wheel drive?

**Yes, all 2025 and newer Toyota Camry models are exclusively hybrid.** Toyota discontinued the traditional gasoline-only engine options, making the entire Camry lineup hybrid-powered. This represents a major shift in the model's history, prioritizing fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.

**Powertrain Specifications:**

**Front-Wheel Drive (FWD):**
- 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with electric motors
- Combined output: 225 horsepower
- Fuel economy: Up to 53 city / 50 highway MPG (LE trim)

**All-Wheel Drive (AWD):**
- Same 2.5-liter engine plus an additional rear electric motor
- Combined output: 232 horsepower
- Available on all trim levels for the first time in Camry history
- Electronic On-Demand AWD system
- Fuel economy: 1-4 MPG lower than FWD depending on trim

The fifth-generation Toyota Hybrid System (THS 5) provides seamless power delivery and impressive efficiency, making the Camry one of the most fuel-efficient midsize sedans on the market.

### How reliable is the Toyota Camry?

The Toyota Camry is exceptionally reliable, consistently ranking among the most dependable vehicles in its class. The Camry has earned a **4.0 out of 5.0 reliability rating from RepairPal**, ranking **3rd out of 24** in the midsize car category.

**Key Reliability Metrics:**
- Average annual repair cost: $388 (well below the segment average)
- Lower-than-average frequency of repairs
- Lower-than-average severity of repairs
- Strong owner satisfaction ratings

Consumer Reports has historically given the Camry excellent reliability scores, with some model years receiving perfect 5 out of 5 predicted reliability ratings. Toyota as a brand ranked #2 for reliability among 26 car brands according to Consumer Reports, with a score of 74.

The Camry's reputation for longevity is well-earned, with many owners reporting trouble-free ownership well beyond 200,000 miles with proper maintenance.

[Read RepairPal's Camry reliability ratings](https://repairpal.com/reliability/toyota/camry)

### Does the Toyota Camry support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year and trim level:

**2025+ Models:**
- LE and SE trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 8-inch touchscreen
- XLE and XSE trims: **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via 12.3-inch touchscreen

**2021-2024 Models:**
- All trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- 8-inch or 9-inch touchscreen depending on trim

**2018-2020 Models:**
- All trims: **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Available through Entune 3.0 system

**2015-2017 Models:**
- Limited or no smartphone integration support
- Aftermarket solutions available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.

### What safety features come standard on the Toyota Camry?

All 2025 Toyota Camry models come standard with **Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 (TSS 3.0)**, Toyota's comprehensive suite of advanced driver assistance systems. The 2025 Camry earned the **IIHS Top Safety Pick+** award, the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety's highest safety accolade.

**Toyota Safety Sense 3.0 includes:**

- **Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection**: Detects vehicles, pedestrians, bicyclists, and motorcyclists, providing automatic emergency braking if a collision is imminent.

- **Full-Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control**: Maintains a set speed and following distance from the vehicle ahead, with four adjustable following distance settings.

- **Lane Tracing Assist**: Helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane with active steering assistance. The TSS 3.0 version includes Emergency Driving Stop System (EDSS) that can bring the vehicle to a stop if the driver becomes unresponsive.

- **Road Sign Assist**: Recognizes and displays important road signs including speed limits, stop signs, and yield signs on the instrument cluster.

- **Automatic High Beams**: Automatically toggles between high and low beams based on surrounding traffic.

- **Proactive Driving Assist**: Provides gentle braking and steering assistance to help maintain safe following distances and navigate curves.

**Additional Standard Safety Features:**
- Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert
- 10 airbags
- Toyota's Star Safety System (VSC, TRAC, ABS, EBD, BA, Smart Stop Technology)

The TSS 3.0 system features upgraded cameras and radar sensors for improved detection range and accuracy compared to previous generations.
