# About the Toyota 4Runner

The Toyota 4Runner is a body-on-frame midsize SUV that has earned a reputation as one of the most capable and reliable off-road vehicles available. First introduced in 1984, the 4Runner combines rugged construction, proven reliability, and serious off-road capabilities with everyday practicality. The 2025 model year brings a complete redesign with updated powertrains and modern technology while maintaining the 4Runner's legendary off-road prowess.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What trim levels are available for the 4Runner?

The 4Runner lineup varies between model years:

**2024 Model Year:**
- **SR5**: Entry-level trim available in RWD or 4WD with essential features
- **TRD Sport**: Sport-oriented styling with enhanced on-road handling
- **SR5 Premium**: Adds premium comfort features over the base SR5
- **TRD Off-Road**: Purpose-built for off-road capability with locking rear differential and crawl control
- **TRD Off-Road Premium**: Enhanced TRD Off-Road with additional comfort features
- **Limited**: Luxury-focused trim with premium materials and technology
- **TRD Pro**: Top-tier off-road variant with Fox shocks, unique styling, and maximum capability

**2025 Model Year (All-New Generation):**
- **SR5**: Redesigned base trim with new turbocharged engine
- **TRD Sport**: Performance-oriented on-road variant
- **TRD Sport Premium**: Enhanced TRD Sport with premium features
- **TRD Off-Road**: Core off-road focused trim
- **TRD Off-Road Premium**: Premium off-road variant
- **Limited**: Luxury-focused model
- **Platinum**: New top luxury trim
- **TRD Pro**: Ultimate off-road performance model
- **Trailhunter**: All-new trim designed for slow-speed technical off-roading with 33-inch tires and enhanced ground clearance

### What engine options are available?

Engine options depend on model year:

**2024 Model Year:**
All 2024 4Runner models come with a 4.0-liter V6 engine producing 270 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Fuel economy is EPA-rated at 16 MPG city and 19 MPG highway (17 MPG combined) across all configurations.

**2025 Model Year (Redesigned):**
The 2025 4Runner introduces two new turbocharged powertrains:

- **i-Force 2.4L Turbo**: Turbocharged 2.4-liter four-cylinder producing 278 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque with an 8-speed automatic transmission

The new powertrains deliver improved fuel efficiency and performance compared to the outgoing V6.

### How reliable is the Toyota 4Runner?

The Toyota 4Runner has an excellent reputation for reliability and longevity. RepairPal gives the 4Runner a reliability rating of 4.0 out of 5.0, ranking it 9th out of 26 midsize SUVs. The model is known for lower-than-average ownership costs and exceptional durability.

Consumer Reports consistently rates the 4Runner highly for reliability, with predicted reliability scores above average for recent model years. The 4Runner's body-on-frame construction and proven powertrain contribute to its ability to last well beyond 200,000 miles with proper maintenance.

Owner satisfaction ratings are particularly strong, with Kelley Blue Book reporting that 90% of 4Runner owners recommend the vehicle, citing reliability and quality as its strongest features.

### What off-road capabilities does the 4Runner offer?

The 4Runner is engineered for serious off-road performance with multiple capability-enhancing features:

**Four-Wheel Drive Systems:**
- Part-time 4WD system with electronically controlled two-speed transfer case (high and low range)
- Available full-time 4WD on select trims
- Locking rear differential on TRD Off-Road and above

**Off-Road Technologies:**
- **Multi-Terrain Select (MTS)**: Five selectable drive modes optimized for different terrain types (Rock, Mogul, Loose Rock, Sand/Mud, and Mud/Sand)
- **Crawl Control (CRAWL)**: Off-road cruise control that maintains steady speed on steep inclines and technical terrain
- **Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS)**: Available hydraulic suspension system that improves wheel articulation (TRD Pro and select trims)

**Ground Clearance and Capability:**
- 9.1-9.6 inches ground clearance depending on trim
- Approach angle: Up to 33 degrees (TRD Pro/Trailhunter)
- Departure angle: Up to 26 degrees
- Body-on-frame construction for exceptional durability and articulation
- Available Bilstein or Fox shocks on off-road trims

The TRD Pro and new Trailhunter models represent the pinnacle of 4Runner off-road capability with upgraded suspension, larger tires, and enhanced protection.

### What is Toyota Safety Sense?

All 2020 and newer Toyota 4Runner models come standard with Toyota Safety Sense P (TSS-P), a comprehensive suite of advanced driver assistance systems:

**Core TSS-P Features:**

- **Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection (PCS w/PD)**: Uses camera and radar to detect potential frontal collisions with vehicles and pedestrians, providing warnings and automatic emergency braking if needed

- **Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist**: Monitors lane markings and alerts drivers to unintentional lane drift, with available steering assistance to help keep the vehicle centered

- **Automatic High Beams (AHB)**: Automatically toggles between high and low beams based on surrounding traffic and lighting conditions

- **Dynamic Radar Cruise Control (DRCC)**: Adaptive cruise control that maintains a preset following distance from vehicles ahead using radar and camera technology

**Additional Safety Features:**
- Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert (available on most trims)
- Standard eight airbags including driver and front passenger knee airbags
- Trailer Sway Control

Toyota Safety Sense P is standard across all 4Runner trims starting with the 2020 model year, providing peace of mind both on and off the road.

### What is the towing capacity of the 4Runner?

The 4Runner offers robust towing capability suitable for outdoor adventures:

**2024 Model Year (4.0L V6):**
- Maximum towing capacity: 5,000 pounds when properly equipped
- Requires Towing Package (integrated trailer brake controller, heavy-duty radiator, upgraded alternator)

**2025 Model Year (Turbocharged Engines):**
- i-Force 2.4L Turbo: Expected similar or improved towing capacity
- i-Force Max Hybrid: Enhanced towing with 465 lb-ft of torque providing strong pulling power

The 4Runner's body-on-frame construction and available trailer sway control make it well-suited for towing travel trailers, boat trailers, and equipment trailers. For optimal towing performance and stability, Toyota recommends distributing trailer weight properly and using a weight-distribution hitch for trailers approaching maximum capacity.
