# About the Toyota 4Runner

The Toyota 4Runner is a body-on-frame midsize SUV that has earned a reputation as one of the most capable and reliable off-road vehicles available. The 2025 model year brings a complete redesign with updated powertrains and modern technology while maintaining the 4Runner's legendary off-road prowess.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the 4Runner support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year:

**2020-2024 Model Years:**
- Wired Apple CarPlay (USB connection required)
- Wired Android Auto (USB connection required)
- 8-inch touchscreen with Entune 3.0 infotainment system

**2025 Model Year (Redesigned):**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay
- **Wireless** Android Auto
- 8-inch touchscreen on base configurations or 14-inch touchscreen on higher configurations
- Multiple USB-C charging ports
- Digital key functionality

For 2020-2024 models with wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available to add wireless functionality to the existing CarPlay/Android Auto system.
