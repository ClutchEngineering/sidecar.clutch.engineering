# About the Ram 1500

The Ram 1500 is a full-size pickup truck that has been a cornerstone of the American truck market since 1981. Known for its blend of capability, comfort, and advanced technology, the Ram 1500 offers a range of configurations to meet diverse needs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Ram 1500 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Ram 1500 supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability and connectivity type varying by model year.

**2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via Uconnect 5 system
- 8.4-inch, 12-inch, or available 14.5-inch touchscreen depending on configuration
- The new 14.5-inch screen is standard on Limited, Longhorn, and Tungsten trims
- USB-C ports for charging and connectivity

**2022-2024 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via Uconnect 5 system
- 8.4-inch or 12-inch touchscreen depending on configuration
- USB-C ports for charging
- Automatic connection when paired device is in range

**2018-2021 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via Uconnect 4 system
- Apple CarPlay first introduced in 2018 on higher trim levels
- Became more widely available across trim levels starting in 2019
- 5-inch, 8.4-inch, or 12-inch touchscreen depending on configuration and model year
- USB-A connection required

**2017 and Earlier:**
- Factory Apple CarPlay and Android Auto **not available**
- Equipped with earlier Uconnect systems without smartphone integration
- Aftermarket upgrade modules available that can add CarPlay/Android Auto functionality

For 2018-2021 models with wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters can be added by plugging into the existing USB port, converting the system to wireless operation without vehicle modification.
