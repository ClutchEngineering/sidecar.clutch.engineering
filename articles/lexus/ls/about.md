# About the Lexus LS

The Lexus LS is the flagship luxury sedan of the Lexus brand. First introduced in 1989 as the LS 400, the LS established Lexus as a serious competitor in the luxury sedan market and has represented the pinnacle of the brand's commitment to quality, refinement, and innovation across five generations.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Lexus LS support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support on the Lexus LS varies by model year, with significant improvements introduced in the 2023 model year.

**2023-2026 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all models
- 12.3-inch touchscreen display
- Lexus Interface multimedia system with intuitive touch and voice controls
- Dual Bluetooth phone connectivity
- The 2023 model year marked a major update with the introduction of wireless connectivity

**2019-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard across all models
- 12.3-inch touchscreen display
- USB cable connection required
- Apple CarPlay became standard equipment starting with the 2019 model year

**2018 Models:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Some 2018 models were eligible for a dealer-installed upgrade to add wired Apple CarPlay
- Upgrade availability varied by build date and market

**Wireless Connectivity Note:**

For 2019-2022 models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.
