# About the Chrysler 300

The Chrysler 300 is a full-size sedan known for its bold, distinctive styling and powerful performance. Production of the Chrysler 300 ended in December 2023, with 2023 being the final model year.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Chrysler 300 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support varies by model year:

**2017-2023 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Available through Uconnect 4C system with 8.4-inch touchscreen
- Standard across most configurations
- Requires compatible USB cable for connection

**2016 and Earlier Models:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions and replacement head units available for adding connectivity

The Uconnect 4C system in 2017-2023 models provides wired smartphone integration only. For models with wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without any modification to the vehicle.
