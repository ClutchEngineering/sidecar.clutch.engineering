# About the Nissan Patrol

The Nissan Patrol is a legendary full-size SUV with over 70 years of heritage dating back to 1951. Built on a rugged body-on-frame platform, the Patrol has earned a global reputation as one of the most capable and durable off-road vehicles ever produced.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Nissan Patrol support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support on the Nissan Patrol varies significantly by model year:

**2024-2025 Model Years:**

- **Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto** come standard across all trim levels
- 2025 models feature the new 28.6-inch Monolith display system with twin 14.3-inch touchscreens
- 2024 Australian models received a major infotainment upgrade with a 12.3-inch touchscreen and wireless smartphone integration
- Multiple USB-C ports for charging and data connectivity

**2010-2023 Model Years:**

The Patrol did **not** come with factory-installed Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support during these model years. However, several aftermarket solutions are available:

- Aftermarket plug-and-play integration modules compatible with 2010-2023 Y62 Patrol models
- Professional installation services can add CarPlay and Android Auto functionality while retaining factory features
- Solutions available for Series 4 and Series 5 Patrol variants (2017-2023)

**Important Note:** For pre-2024 models, verify your specific build date when considering aftermarket upgrades, as internal system changes (particularly in late 2020 and beyond) may affect compatibility with certain integration modules.

The addition of wireless smartphone integration in 2024 brought the Patrol in line with modern connectivity expectations, making it easier to access navigation, music, and communication features while on the road.
