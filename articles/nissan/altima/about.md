# About the Nissan Altima

The Nissan Altima is a midsize sedan known for its comfort, efficiency, and value. First introduced in 1992, the Altima has become one of Nissan's best-selling models in North America, offering a practical blend of features and modern connectivity options.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Nissan Altima support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Nissan Altima supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability varying by model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard with 8-inch touchscreen
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available with optional 12.3-inch touchscreen upgrade
- The 12.3-inch display also includes wireless phone charging pad and Wi-Fi hotspot capability

**2023 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard on most configurations with 8-inch touchscreen
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available as optional upgrade with 12.3-inch touchscreen
- Base configurations with 7-inch touchscreen do not include CarPlay or Android Auto support

**2019-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- USB cable connection required
- Standard on most configurations
- 8-inch touchscreen with NissanConnect infotainment system

**2018 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 2018 was the first model year to offer factory Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- USB cable connection required

**2016-2017 Models:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket head unit replacements available to add smartphone integration

**2015 and Earlier:**
- No Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
