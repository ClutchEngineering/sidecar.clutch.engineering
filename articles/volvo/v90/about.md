# About the Volvo V90

The Volvo V90 is a midsize luxury wagon that combines Scandinavian design with practicality and premium features. Introduced in 2017, the V90 represents Volvo's commitment to the station wagon format, offering both standard and Cross Country variants.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Volvo V90 support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Apple CarPlay and Android Auto support on the Volvo V90 varies significantly by model year and infotainment system:

**2022-2025 Models (Android Automotive OS with Google Built-in):**

- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 9-inch vertically oriented touchscreen
- Android Automotive OS with built-in Google apps (Google Maps, Google Assistant, Google Play Store)
- Apple CarPlay introduced with the 2022 model year when Volvo rolled out the new Google-based infotainment system
- Android Auto support added via software update
- Two USB ports
- Wireless device charging available
- Note: The V90 Cross Country is the only variant available in the U.S. market from 2022 onward

**2017-2021 Models (Sensus Connect System):**

- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available via dealer installation
- 9-inch portrait-style touchscreen with Sensus Connect infotainment system
- Apple CarPlay and Android Auto were not factory-installed on all models and often required dealer installation as an upgrade
- USB connection required for smartphone integration
- Standard V90 wagon was discontinued in the U.S. after the 2021 model year

**Wireless Connectivity Note:**

Neither generation of the V90 includes native wireless Apple CarPlay or Android Auto from the factory. For owners seeking wireless connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.

For the latest specifications on the V90 Cross Country, visit the [official Volvo Cars website](https://www.volvocars.com/us/cars/v90-cross-country/).
