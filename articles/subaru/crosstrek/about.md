# About the Subaru Crosstrek

The Subaru Crosstrek is a compact crossover SUV that combines the practicality of a small SUV with Subaru's renowned all-wheel-drive capability. Since its introduction in 2013, the Crosstrek has become one of Subaru's most popular models, offering excellent ground clearance, modern technology features, and versatile utility.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Subaru Crosstrek support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Subaru Crosstrek supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability varying by model year and configuration.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Base trim**: Dual 7-inch touchscreen with **wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- **Premium, Sport, Limited, and Wilderness trims**: 11.6-inch touchscreen with **wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- Models with the 11.6-inch screen also include wireless charging

**2023 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- 8-inch touchscreen display
- Standard across most configurations

**2019-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- Base models: 6.5-inch touchscreen
- Premium, Limited, Hybrid, and Sport models: 8.0-inch touchscreen
- Standard across most configurations

**2018 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 6.5-inch or 8.0-inch touchscreen depending on configuration
- First model year to introduce CarPlay and Android Auto support

**2017 and Earlier:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions may be available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
