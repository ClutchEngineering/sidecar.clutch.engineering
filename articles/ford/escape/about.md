# About the Ford Escape

The Ford Escape is a compact SUV that blends versatility, efficiency, and advanced technology features. First introduced in 2000, the Escape has become one of Ford's most popular SUV models, offering practical everyday transportation with modern connectivity features.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Ford Escape support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Ford Escape supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability varying by model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via SYNC 4 system
- 8-inch touchscreen standard across all configurations
- Optional 13.2-inch touchscreen available on most configurations
- Enhanced voice recognition and faster system response times

**2023 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via SYNC 4 system
- First model year to introduce wireless connectivity
- 8-inch touchscreen standard, with optional 13.2-inch touchscreen on higher configurations

**2017-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via SYNC 3 system
- USB cable connection required
- The 2017 Escape was the first 2017 Ford vehicle to feature Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 8-inch touchscreen standard on most configurations

**2016 and Earlier:**
- No Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions may be available

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.

The Escape's infotainment system provides seamless smartphone integration, making it easy to access navigation, music, messages, and apps while on the road.
