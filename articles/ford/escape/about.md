# About the Ford Escape

The Ford Escape is a compact SUV that has been a popular choice in the crossover segment since 1999. Known for its versatility, efficiency, and modern technology features, the Escape offers a blend of practicality and connectivity that appeals to a wide range of drivers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Ford Escape support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, the Ford Escape supports both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, with availability varying by model year.

**2024-2025 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via SYNC 4
- 8-inch touchscreen standard across all configurations
- Optional 13.2-inch touchscreen available on higher configurations
- Enhanced connectivity features with faster response times

**2023 Models:**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto via SYNC 4
- 8-inch touchscreen standard on base configurations
- 13.2-inch touchscreen available on higher configurations
- First model year to introduce wireless connectivity in the Escape lineup

**2017-2022 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (USB connection required)
- SYNC 3 infotainment system
- 8-inch touchscreen standard on most configurations
- The 2017 model year was the first to include CarPlay and Android Auto support

**2016 and Earlier:**
- No Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- Aftermarket solutions available for older models

For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality without modification to the vehicle.
