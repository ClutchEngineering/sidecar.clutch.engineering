# About the Ford F-150 Lightning

The Ford F-150 Lightning is an all-electric version of America's best-selling pickup truck. Introduced for the 2022 model year, the Lightning combines the F-150's legendary capability with electric vehicle technology, offering impressive power, innovative features, and advanced connectivity.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Ford F-150 Lightning support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, all Ford F-150 Lightning model years support wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto as standard equipment.

**All Model Years (2022-2025):**
- **Wireless** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto (standard across all configurations)
- SYNC 4A infotainment system with portrait-oriented touchscreen
- 12-inch touchscreen standard on Pro and XLT configurations
- 15.5-inch touchscreen standard on Lariat and Platinum configurations
- USB-C ports for charging and connectivity

The F-150 Lightning's wireless smartphone integration eliminates the need for cables, automatically connecting when you enter the vehicle. The large vertical touchscreen provides an excellent interface for navigation, music, messages, and apps.

For the latest specifications and features, visit the [official Ford F-150 Lightning page](https://www.ford.com/trucks/f150-lightning/).
