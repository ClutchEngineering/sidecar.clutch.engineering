# About the Ford Taurus

The Ford Taurus is a full-size sedan that was produced by Ford from 1986 to 2019. Originally introduced as a revolutionary aerodynamic design that helped revitalize Ford in the 1980s, the Taurus became one of America's best-selling sedans before production ended in March 2019.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does the Ford Taurus support Apple CarPlay and Android Auto?

Yes, Ford Taurus models equipped with SYNC 3 support both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, though the feature was not available across all model years.

**2017-2019 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- 8-inch touchscreen display with SYNC 3
- USB cable connection required (Lightning to USB for iPhone)
- Available on most configurations

**2016 Models:**
- **Wired** Apple CarPlay and Android Auto available via software update to SYNC 3 version 2.2
- Could be updated via USB drive, dealer installation, or Wi-Fi connection
- 8-inch touchscreen display on equipped models

**2015 and Earlier:**
- No factory Apple CarPlay or Android Auto support
- These models were equipped with earlier SYNC generations (SYNC 1 or SYNC with MyFord Touch)

The Ford Taurus was discontinued after the 2019 model year as Ford shifted focus to SUVs and trucks. For models with only wired connectivity, aftermarket wireless adapters are available that plug into the existing USB port and enable wireless functionality.

For more information about the Ford Taurus, visit the [Ford Taurus Wikipedia page](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ford_Taurus).
